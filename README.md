
# Pharmaceutical Sales & Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project analyses pharmaceutical sales performance across eight European countries, 
exploring revenue trends, product categories, geographic distribution, pharmacy 
segmentation, and promotional impact to identify key revenue drivers and strategic 
growth opportunities.

## Objectives
a.	Analyse revenue trends across time, product categories, and regions
b.	Identify top-performing pharmacies, store sizes, and geographic markets
c.	Evaluate the impact of promotional vs non-promotional sales
d.	Provide strategic recommendations to improve profitability

## Tools Used
- Power BI — data cleaning, preparation, data visualization and dashboard reporting

## Key Findings
a.	Prescription products generate the highest revenue contribution
b.	Non-promotional sales drive 89.45% of total revenue
c.	Urban pharmacies outperform rural pharmacies significantly
d.	Medium-sized stores are the strongest revenue drivers
e.	Germany, France, and Italy are the top-performing countries

## Limitations
a.	Dataset covers only 8 European countries and may not represent global trends
b.	Promotional impact analysis is limited to available sales data
c.	Store-level operational costs are not included in this analysis
d.	Data does not capture patient demographics or prescription frequency
